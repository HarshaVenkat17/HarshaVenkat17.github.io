Harsha 2.0  is a virtual voice assistant designed for chrome and internet explorer using basic HTML, CSS and Javascript. Its aim is to provide flexibility in operation. The original version "Harsha" is made for Windows PC and is more flexible than the present version due to availability of more functionalities and areas to explore. The current version Harsha 2.0 has the following functionalities:

  1. Tells date and time.
  2. Gives weather details of any particular area or user's location even if user's location is not mentioned.
  3. Opens youtube and google.
  4. Search something in youtube and google using any form of queries given by user.
  5. Opens Amazon, Flipkart, Netflix and Amazon Prime.
  6. Orders food from Zomato and Swiggy.
  7. Book tickets for movies, bus, plane or to book a cab or taxi.
  8. Opens Skype and WhatsApp.
  9. Opens calculator.
  10. Opens MS-Office and Google applications.
  11. Can give information on sports and news.
  12. Plays songs
  13. Sets timer and stopwatch.
  14. Finds location of a place in Google maps.
  15. To navigate or show directions from one place to another.
  16. Closes on asking to "Close","Exit" or "Quit".
* Do not forget to allow popups if you want to open another website. Go to Settings → Site settings → Popups and redirects → Allow. Now click on "Add" and add the url.
